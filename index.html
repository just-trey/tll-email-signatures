Tuckahoe Little League's Email Signatures

This page was created to allow TLL Board members to have a consistent email signature in their email correspondence. If you see an error or need a new signature created contact trey.wentz@tuckahoe.org.

Instructions:

Note: Perform these steps on a computer in your web browser and not your phone or tablet

  1. Find your signature below and click the copy button next to the signature. This will copy the signature to your computerâ€™s clipboard.
  2. Open your Tuckahoe gmail account.
  3. Click the settings gear in the upper right hand corner of Gmail and select settings from the menu that will appear.
  4. Ensure you are on the general tab.
  5. Scroll down the page and look for Signature
  6. Right click on in the box next to the signature area and select paste
  7. Click the checkbox next to Insert this signature before quoted text in replies and remove the "--" line that precedes it.
  8. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and select save changes.
